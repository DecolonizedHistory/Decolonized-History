World History

I hope that we can all understand that the world existed before humans because it is about 4.5 billion years old, and the earliest fossils of life date back to 3.7 billion years ago. Homo Sapiens have been walking around since at least 300,000 years ago, but what we would call modern humans only really occurred 164,000 years ago.

Which is where Human History technically starts. Very little of Human History is documented, and within those documents very few tell the full story. 

To start the exploration of human history we start with the land

World History Through Maps

To understand human history around the world, you must have an accurate map. Turning a spherical planet into a flat map that is 100% accurate is not actually possible, but AuthaGraph designed by Hajime Narukawa gets very close.
